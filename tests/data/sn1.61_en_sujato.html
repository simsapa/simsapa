  • 7. Oppressed 7. Addhavagga ⧫ (Addhavagga → anvavaggo (bj); andhavaggo (sya-all, pts2ed))

Name Nāmasutta

“What oppresses everything? “Kiṁsu sabbaṁ addhabhavi, ⧫ (addhabhavi → anvabhavi (bj); andhabhavi (sya-all)) What is nothing bigger than? kismā bhiyyo na vijjati; What is the one thing Kissassu ekadhammassa, that has everything under its sway?” sabbeva vasamanvagū”ti. ⧫ (vasamanvagū”ti → vasamaddhagū (mr))

“Name oppresses everything. “Nāmaṁ sabbaṁ addhabhavi, Nothing’s bigger than name. nāmā bhiyyo na vijjati; Name is the one thing Nāmassa ekadhammassa, that has everything under its sway.” sabbeva vasamanvagū”ti.
